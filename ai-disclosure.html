2. The Three AI Features

  • AutoName — generates a suggested title for a scanned document.
  • AutoSort — suggests a category (for example, Receipts, Invoices, Medical, Identity, Legal, Financial, Work, Personal) for a scanned document.
  • Recall — answers questions about your scanned documents using their extracted text content.

3. What is Transmitted, and When

AI features do not run on your device. When you invoke an AI feature, the following happens:

  1. The App prepares a payload on your device. For AutoName and AutoSort this is one page from the document, downscaled to a small image, or a short text snippet derived from on-device OCR. For Recall it is the question you typed or spoke, the most relevant text extracted from your documents, and a brief recent chat history.
  2. The payload is transmitted over HTTPS to a secure proxy operated by us and hosted on Cloudflare. The proxy is authenticated and accepts requests only from the App.
  3. The proxy forwards the payload to the Google Gemini API for processing.
  4. The model's response is returned through the proxy to the App and rendered on your device.
  5. Request and response bodies are not retained by our proxy after the request completes. Limited operational data (such as a short-term value used for abuse prevention) is processed transiently and not used to identify individual users.

4. Third Parties Involved

  • Cloudflare operates the network on which our proxy runs. Cloudflare may process technical metadata (such as IP address) according to its own policies.
  • Google runs the Gemini API that produces the AI output. Google processes content under its API terms and Gemini data-use policies. We have configured the App to use the Gemini API rather than Google AI Studio's consumer surface; this is the path on which Google's documented terms state that user content is not used to train models.

We do not control these third parties and do not assume their obligations. Any change in their terms or behaviour may affect what happens to content you submit; we may change AI providers at any time on notice through this Disclosure.

5. What is Not Transmitted

  • Documents that you do not invoke an AI feature on remain on your device only.
  • Voice for the Recall feature is transcribed on-device by Apple's speech-recognition framework; the audio never leaves the device.
  • Your real name, email address, Apple ID, persistent user identifier, or location is not attached to any AI request by us.
  • Biometric data (Face ID / Touch ID) is handled entirely by iOS and never leaves your device.
