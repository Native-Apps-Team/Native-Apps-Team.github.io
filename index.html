Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a document?

Tap the scan button on the home screen to open the camera. Point your iPhone at the document — the app will detect the edges automatically. Tap the shutter to capture, then tap Done to save.

What export formats are supported?

Free users can export as JPEG. Pro subscribers can export as PDF or DOCX. Tap the share icon on any document to choose your format.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Tap the Pro button on the home screen or during export to view subscription options. Pro is available as a weekly or yearly subscription, with a 7-day free trial for new subscribers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → NativeScan → Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions directly in the App Store.

Why is the free plan limited to 2 pages per scan?

The free plan includes 2 pages per scan and JPEG export. Upgrading to Pro removes the page limit and unlocks PDF and DOCX export, along with future Pro features.

Where are my scanned documents stored?

All documents are stored locally on your device. NativeScan does not upload your files to any server. Your documents are private and only accessible on the device you scanned them on.

How do I delete a document?

In the Scanned Docs list, swipe left on any document to reveal the Delete option. Tap Delete to remove it permanently.
